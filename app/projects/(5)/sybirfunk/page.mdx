## Technologies used

- TypeScript
- Phaser

## Play online

[https://sybirfunk.bodgingbear.dev](https://sybirfunk.bodgingbear.dev/)

## Controls

- Move using **_arrow keys_**
- Shoot with **_space bar_**
- Shop with **_numerics_**:
  1. ammo
  2. sasha
  3. boris
  4. vodka
- Activate Boris (fat soviet soldier) with **Q**
- Drink vodka with **D**
